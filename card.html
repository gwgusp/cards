Reeds Lake

  • Location: East Grand Rapids, Michigan, 49506
  • Website: East Grand Rapids - Reeds Lake
  • Category: Parks and Trails
  • Recommended by: Gina R

Reeds lake is located next to Gaslight Village in East Grand Rapids, MI. It has a 4.2 mile trail made up of paved trails, boardwalks, and sidewalks, as well as a small park and numerous boardwalks for fishing.
